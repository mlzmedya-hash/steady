Steady — Privacy Policy

Steady: Blood Sugar Tracker · Last updated: June 21, 2026
The short version: Steady is local-first. Everything you log is stored in an encrypted database on your own device. By default nothing is uploaded to any server, there are no advertising or analytics trackers, no account is required, and we never sell your data.

Not medical advice

Steady records, organizes and reminds — it does not diagnose, give insulin or medication doses, predict glucose, or alarm on glucose levels. Nothing in the app is medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your treatment and care decisions.

What data the app handles

Steady stores the information you choose to enter, such as glucose readings, meals/carbohydrates, insulin and medication logs, ketones, lab results, blood pressure, reminders, supply inventory, an optional emergency card, and your profile and target settings. This is health-related information and we treat it accordingly.

Where your data is stored

All of the above is stored in an encrypted database on your device (SQLCipher). The encryption key is held in your device's secure keychain. By default the data never leaves your phone, and Steady works fully offline. We do not operate a server that receives your readings.

No account required

You can use every core feature without creating an account or being online. If optional cloud sync is offered in the future, it is strictly opt-in, off by default, and would sync summaries rather than your raw readings — and this policy will be updated before any such feature is enabled.

No trackers, no ads, no sale of data

Steady contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We do not track your behaviour across apps or websites, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Permissions we may request

  • Notifications — to deliver the reminders you set (e.g. test or take medication). These are prompts, never glucose alarms.
  • Camera — used only to scan a food barcode so carbs can be filled in. No photo is taken or stored.
  • Apple Health / Health Connect (optional) — if you allow it, to import glucose and meter readings. You can turn this off at any time in your system settings.

Backups and exports you control

You can create an encrypted backup protected by your own passphrase, or export your data as CSV/JSON/PDF to share with your doctor. These actions only happen when you initiate them. An encrypted backup cannot be opened without your passphrase, which we cannot recover — please keep it safe.

Deleting your data

You own your data. You can delete individual entries at any time, and removing the app from your device removes its local database. Because we do not store your data on a server, there is no server-side copy for us to retain.

Children

Steady is not directed to children under the age required by your local law to consent to data processing, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. A parent or guardian may use the app to record information on a child's behalf, on their own device and under their control.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
