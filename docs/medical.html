Steady is for observation and organization only. It is not a medical device, and nothing in the app is medical advice.

What Steady does — and does not — do

Steady helps you record, organize and remember diabetes-related information. It does not:

  • diagnose any condition;
  • recommend or calculate insulin or medication doses;
  • predict future glucose values;
  • alarm or alert you about real-time glucose levels;
  • replace professional medical care.

Always consult a professional

Decisions about your treatment — including medication, insulin, diet, and responses to high or low readings — must be made with a qualified healthcare professional. Never disregard professional medical advice, or delay seeking it, because of something you saw in this app.

Patterns and observations are retrospective

Any "patterns", trends, or meal-impact observations in the app describe your past recorded data. They are informational observations, not predictions, recommendations, or clinical findings.

In an emergency

If you think you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately. Do not rely on the app.
